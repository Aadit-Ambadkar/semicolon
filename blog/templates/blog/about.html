What is Semicolon Math?

Semicolon Math is a platform that tries to be the math version of Codeforces. We offer various contests.

All problems on this website are licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International License.

The code for this website is available on Github (coming soon) under a GPL license.

Privacy Notice

We store your username and a hashed and salted version of your password. However, you should use a unique and strong password for this website for security reasons.

We also store the answers you submit to a problem, your rating, the scores of the contests you participate in, and during a contest, the time you submitted a problem in order to calculate your score.

We do not knowingly log any other personal data, such as your email.

Contest Rules

We try to simulate the AMC

  • You CAN ONLY use scratch paper, pencils, erasers, colored pencils/pens/markers, rulers, and compasses
  • You CANNOT use protractors or graph paper
  • You CANNOT use any calculator (online or offline) or any computing services (like WolframAlpha). You also cannot write any code to solve the problems. All problems must be solved by YOU and not a computer.
  • You CANNOT discuss the problems with any other person when the contest is RUNNING. ONLY SOLVE THE PROBLEMS BY YOURSELF.
  • If you have a question DURING the contest, please DM mathmastersd on AoPS. DO NOT ASK ON THE FORUM. DO NOT ask how to solve a problem, or the answer to a problem.
  • Failure to follow the rules MAY result in your account getting DELETED.

If you know someone who cheated, please contact us with information. It is difficult to get around cheating, so please do not be discouraged. The point of these contests is to have fun, at the end of the day!
